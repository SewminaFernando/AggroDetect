Empower Your Call Center with Peaceful Conversations

ABOUT US

AggroDetect is a system that is directed by revolutionized call center experience, retrieved from data science methodologies to analyze voice and transcript text. The system enables distinguishing between Aggressive and Non-Aggressive customers to provide tailored and exceptional call center experience to the customers. The call center agents will be empowered by the system to enhance customer satisfaction while onboarding aggressive customers with a careful and deeper understanding.

Services

Aggressiveness Detection By Voice

Our system uses advanced technology to analyze the tone, volume, and choice of words in someone's speech. It then compares these features to known patterns associated with aggressive behavior. If it detects similarities, it flags the speech as potentially aggressive.

Automated Response System

Our automated response system provides responses to customers and helps them with questions or requests they have. It understands what customers say and gives helpful answers or guides them to the right place. It learns over time to get better at understanding and helping customers.

Agent Routing

Our system helps streamline the handling of requests by ensuring they are directed to the most appropriate department and agent, improving efficiency and customer experience.
